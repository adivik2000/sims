Release Notes for SIMS

  • Changes since May 17 Release
    • Team Consultation Forms are available at http://sims-wip.vegantech.com (wip is work in progress.)
    • New interventions should again be automatically preset to the recommendations for frequency and time length.
    • Districts can now make a copy of the statewide checklist to use in the builder. Districts can also now recommendations to their checklists in the builder.
    • Display appropriate messages when there are no tiers when attempting to checklists or principal overrides. Display a message when attempting a principal override without any principals.
    • Adjusted formatting of checklist, fixed some issues when viewing in IE6 at 1024x768.
    • Setup interventions ending this week email.
    • When adding new progress monitor scores, the new graph can be previewed. They now display benchmarks and are constrained to the maximum and minimum in the progress monitor definition. Graphs are now in date ascending order.
